Lost Sisters
by oyinade

Synopsis
Simi's anguished cries pierced the air, her gaze fixed on her phone as she teetered on the edge of consciousness. A call flashed on the screen, but she could only watch, her strength waning with each passing moment. In a sudden burst, Jeff burst into the room. His feet froze as he beheld Simi lying amidst a pool of blood, speechless and stunned to his core. The echoes of her distraught voice, hinting at self-harm, reverberated in his mind. “Why? Why?”
