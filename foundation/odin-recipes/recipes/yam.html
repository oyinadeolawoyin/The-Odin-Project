In North America, a yam is the same thing as a sweet potato, although in most parts of the world these words refer to two completely different vegetables.

ingredients

  • A tuber of puna Yam (white)
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Sugar: optional

Steps

How To Cook Yam

  1. Rinse yam clean thoroughly and place in a large saucepan
  2. Add enough water to cover the yam and add salt
  3. Place on medium heat and cook till yam is soft. Yam is cooked if a fork goes through it easily.
  4. Drain the yam (do this to avoid soggy yam) and serve immediately.
